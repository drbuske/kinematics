The spherical coordinates of Sydney are \((4000,151^{\circ},124^{\circ})\text{.}\) In rectangular coordinates, we use (1.6) to locate Sydney at
\begin{align*} x = \mathstrut \amp 4000\sin(124^{\circ}) \cos(151^{\circ}) \approx -2900 \\ y = \mathstrut \amp 4000 \sin(124^{\circ}) \sin(151^{\circ}) \approx 1608 \\ z = \mathstrut \amp 4000 \cos(124^{\circ}) \approx -2237 \end{align*}
where the units are miles.
