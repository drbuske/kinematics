Remember to convert the meteorological angle \(\phi_{met}=330^{\circ}\) to a mathematical angle \(\phi_{polar} = 270^{\circ}-\phi_{met}\text{.}\)
\begin{align*} \vec{v}_H = \mathstrut \amp (20 \ {\rm{knots}})\cos (270^{\circ}-330^{\circ}) \vec{i} + (20 \ {\rm{knots}})\sin (270^{\circ}-330^{\circ}) \vec{j} \\ \approx \mathstrut \amp (10 \ {\rm{knots}})\vec{i} - (17.3 \ {\rm{knots}})\vec{j} \end{align*}
