Checkpoint 3.1.13. Current Windchill Index.

In Minnesota, we tend to report air temperature in degrees Fahrenheit and wind speed in miles per hour. Using these units of measure, the current formula for the Windchill Index is
\begin{equation*} W(v,T) = 35.74 + 0.6215T -35.75v^{0.16} + 0.4275Tv^{0.16} \end{equation*}
where \(v\) is the wind speed in miles per hour (mph) and \(T\) is the air temperature in degees Fahrenheit (\(^{\circ}F\)). The domain for this function only includes wind speeds \(v\) above 3 mph and air temperatures \(T\) below \(50^{\circ}F\text{.}\)
