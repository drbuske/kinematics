Item 2.1.11.c.
Note that \(\vec{v}_{\rm{mean}} = 8\vec{i} + 8\vec{j}\) and \(\vec{v}_{usp} = 1\vec{j}\text{.}\) This makes \(\vec{v}_{\rm{storm}} = \vec{v}_{\rm{mean}} + \vec{v}_{usp} = 8\vec{i} + 9\vec{j}\text{.}\) The meteorlogical direction will then be given by \(\phi_{met} = 270^{\circ}-\arctan(8/9) \approx 228.4^{\circ}\text{.}\)
