Coriolis acceleration \(\vec{a}_{\rm{Cor}}\) expressed in this coordinate system is then
\begin{align*} -2\vec{\Omega} \times \vec{v} = \mathstrut \amp -2 \det \left( \begin{array}{ccc} \vec{i} \amp \vec{j} \amp \vec{k} \\ 0 \amp \omega \cos \varphi \amp \omega \sin \varphi \\ v_x \amp v_y \amp v_z \end{array} \right) \\ = \mathstrut \amp -2 \left[ (\omega v_z \cos \varphi - \omega v_y \sin \varphi)\vec{i} + \omega v_x \sin \varphi \vec{j} - \omega v_x \cos \varphi \vec{k} \right] \end{align*}
