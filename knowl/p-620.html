We compute
\begin{align*} div \ \vec{F} = \mathstrut \amp \frac{\partial }{\partial x}(x) + \frac{\partial}{\partial y}(2y+1) + \frac{\partial }{\partial z}(2z)\\ = \mathstrut \amp 1+2+2 \\ = \mathstrut \amp 5. \end{align*}
Since \(div \ \vec{F}(0,0,0) = 5 > 0\text{,}\) we conclude that the origin is a source. In fact, every point \((x,y,z)\) in this vector field \(\vec{F}\) is a source.
