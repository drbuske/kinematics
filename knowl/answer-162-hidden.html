First, note that \(\frac{\partial^2}{\partial x^2}(ax+by+cz) = \frac{\partial}{\partial x}(a) = 0\text{.}\) Similarly, \(\frac{\partial^2 }{\partial y^2}(ax+by+cz) = \frac{\partial}{\partial y}(b) = 0\) and \(\frac{\partial^2 }{\partial z^2}(ax+by+cz) = \frac{\partial}{\partial z}(c) = 0\text{.}\) Then,
\begin{equation*} \Delta u(x,y,z) = \frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial x^2} + \frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial y^2} + \frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial z^2} = 0+0+0=0. \end{equation*}
