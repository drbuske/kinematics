Exercise 8. Directions and Vectors.

Suppose that north is the direction of the positive \(y\)-axis and east is the direction of the positive \(x\)-axis.
  1. Describe a mathematical vector \(\vec{v}=a\vec{i}+b\vec{j}\) representing a Southeast wind. That is, determine values of \(a\) and \(b\) giving the resolution of \(\vec{v}\text{.}\)
  2. Give a vector parallel, but not equal to \(\vec{v}\text{.}\)
  3. Give a vector \(\vec{w}\) pointing in the opposite direction of \(\vec{v}\text{.}\) This vector should represent a Northwest wind.
  4. Give a unit vector \(\vec{u}\) representing a Southeast wind.
How many vectors can you find in each case above?
