Item 4.3.6.a.

Based on the figure, a small paddle wheel placed at \((0,1,0)\) would spin counter-clockwise the fastest if the handle is placed in the \(\vec{k}\) direction. So \({\rm{curl}} \ \vec{F}(0,1,0)\) points in the \(\vec{k}\) direction. Similarly, a small paddle wheel placed at \((0,-1,0)\) would spin counter-clockwise the fastest if the handle is placed in the \(-\vec{k}\) direction. So \({\rm{curl}} \ \vec{F}(0,-1,0)\) points in the \(-\vec{k}\) direction.
