Item 3.1.20.b.
\(\frac{\partial C}{\partial T}(5,20) \approx \frac{19-7}{25-15} = 1.2\frac{^{\circ}F}{^{\circ}F} = 1.2\text{;}\) in practical terms, when the wind is blowing at 5 mph and the temperature is \(20^{\circ}F\text{,}\) then it will feel roughly 1.2 degrees Fahrenheit warmer for every 1 degree Fahrenheit the temperature increases - again a rate of change.
