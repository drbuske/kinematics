Item 3.1.3.6.b.
When \(p < 1000\text{,}\) the base of the exponential part of the function \(\theta(T,p)\) is greater than 1 leading to a potential temperature that is greater than the absolute temperature. That is, \(\theta > T\text{.}\) As \(p\) decreases, the potential temperature \(\theta\) increases.
