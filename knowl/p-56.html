Shown in Figure 1.2.22 is in isobaric surface representing 850 mb (millibars). Note that this pressure occurs at varying heights in the atmosphere. For example, where the temperature is warmest, it occurs at a height of 1800 meters. Where the temperature is coolest, 850 mb occurs at a height of 1200 meters. That is, temperatures change from average, to warm, back to average, to cold, and then to average again at the eastern edge of Figure 1.2.22.
