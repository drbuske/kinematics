The gradient \(\nabla u\) of any function \(u(x,y)\) is a vector field that points in the direction of steepest ascent. That, is at each order pair \((x,y)\) in the domain of \(u\text{,}\) the vector \(\nabla u\) points in the direction in which we should travel in order to increase the output of \(u\) at the greatest rate.
