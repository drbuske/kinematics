Item 3.1.3.9.c.
In some instances when a WBGT monitoring device is unavailable, a chart as in Figure 3.1.29 can be used to estimate WBGT. These are estimates and are derived only from using temperature \(T\) (in \(^{\circ}C\)) and relative humidity \(RH\) (as a percent) and the chart accounts for full sunshine and light wind conditions. Depending on the radiant heat load from the sun and the wind, the actual WBGT reading could be different from what is on the chart.
The chart gives \({\rm{WBGT}}(T,RH)\text{.}\) Use a central difference to estimate the value of \(\frac{\partial{\rm{WBGT}}}{\partial T}(30,95)\text{.}\) Be sure include units. Then, interpret the meaning of this value.
