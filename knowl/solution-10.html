Solution 2.3.2.1.

(a) Since \(\vec v\cdot \vec w= ||\vec v||\,||\vec w||\cos \theta\) and \(||\vec v\times\vec w||=||\vec v||\, ||\vec w||\sin\theta\text{,}\)
\begin{equation*} \tan \theta = \frac{\sin\theta}{\cos\theta} = \frac{||\vec v\times\vec w||}{\vec v\cdot \vec w} = \frac{3}{4} = 0.75. \end{equation*}
(b) Then \(\theta = \tan^{-1}(0.75) = 0.643501\text{.}\)
