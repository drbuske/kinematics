Answer 4.3.12.1.

We estimate \(\frac{\Delta v}{\Delta x} \approx \frac{0-10 \ m/s}{100 \ m} = -0.1 \ \frac{1}{s}\) and \(\frac{\Delta u}{\Delta y} \approx \frac{10\cos(45)-10\cos(60) \ m/s}{100 \ m} \approx -0.02 \ \frac{1}{s}\text{.}\) It follows that the vertical component of vorticity is \(\eta = \frac{\partial v}{\partial x} - \frac{\partial u}{\partial y} \approx -0.1 - 0.02 = -0.12 \ 1/s\text{.}\)
