Definition 4.3.1. Geometric Definition of Curl.

The curl of a smooth vector field \(\vec{F}\text{,}\) written \({\rm{curl}} \ \vec{F}\text{,}\) is the vector field with the following properties:
  • The direction of \({\rm{curl}} \ \vec{F}(x,y,z)\) is the direction of \(\vec{n}\) for which circulation density \(circ_{\vec{n}} \vec{F}(x,y,z)\) is the greatest.
  • The magnitude of \({\rm{curl}}\ \vec{F}(x,y,z)\) is the circulation density of \(\vec{F}\) around that direction.
