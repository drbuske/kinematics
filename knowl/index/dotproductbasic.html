Exercise 5. Dot Products of Basic Vectors.

Compute each dot product of basis vectors.
  1. \(\displaystyle \vec{i} \cdot \vec{j}\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \vec{j} \cdot \vec{k}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \vec{k} \cdot \vec{i}\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \vec{i} \cdot \vec{i}\)
Answer.
  1. 0
  2. 0
  3. 0
  4. 1
