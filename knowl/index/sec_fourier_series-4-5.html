A sinusoid is a curve similar to the graph of a sine function, but possibly shifted or stretched horizontally and vertically. The general sinusoid of amplitude \(A\text{,}\) angular frequency \(\omega\text{,}\) phase shift \(\phi/\omega\text{,}\) and vertical shift \(B\) is the graph of
\begin{equation*} f(t) = A\sin(\omega t -\phi)+B. \end{equation*}
