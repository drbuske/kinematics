There are seven basic variables that describe the state \(S\) of the atmosphere:
  • \(T\) (temperature)
  • \(\rho\) (pressure)
  • \(\phi\) (geopotential)
  • \(q\) (humidity/moisture)
  • \(u\) (\(x\)-component of wind)
  • \(v\) (\(y\)-component of wind)
  • \(w\) (\(z\)-component of wind)
That is, \(S = h(T,\rho, \phi, q, u, v, w)\text{.}\)
