Exercise 8. Resolving a Wind Barb.

Resolve the horizontal wind vector \(\vec{v}_H\) shown in Figure 4.1.45 as a wind barb. That is, describe this wind in the form \(a\vec{j} + b\vec{j}\) where \(a\) and \(b\) are in units of knots.
Answer.
\begin{align*} \vec{v}_H = \amp (20 \ {\rm{knots}}) \cos (-30^{\circ}) \vec{i} + (20 \ {\rm{knots}}) \sin (-30^{\circ}) \vec{j} \\ \approx \amp (17.3\ {\rm{knots}})\vec{i} -(10\ {\rm{knots}})\vec{j}\text{.} \end{align*}
