Checkpoint 4.1.15. Wind Barbs and Mathematical Vectors.

The wind barb given in Figure 4.1.16 can be thought of as a mathematical vector \(\vec{v}\text{.}\) What is the mathematical angle \(\phi_{polar}\) for this vector \(\vec{v}\text{?}\) What is the magnitude of this vector?
Answer.
Since \(\phi_{met}\) makes an angle of \(40^{\circ}\) with the positive \(y\)-axis pointing north, the mathematical angle is \(\phi_{polar}=270^{\circ}+40^{\circ}=310^{\circ}\text{;}\) the magnitude of this vector is 35 knots.
