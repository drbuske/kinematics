Exercise 7. Seeing a Cross Product.

Compute \(\vec{v} \times \vec{w}\text{.}\) That is, give the resolution of this product. Then, plot on Figure 4.3.30 the vector \(\displaystyle \frac{1}{6} (\vec{v} \times \vec{w})\text{.}\)
Answer.
We resolve \(\vec{v} = 0\vec{i} -3\vec{j} - 4\vec{k}\) and \(\vec{w} = 0\vec{i} + 3\vec{j} + 0 \vec{k}\text{.}\) So \(\vec{v} \times \vec{w} = 12\vec{i}\text{.}\) This means \(\displaystyle \frac{1}{6}(\vec{v} \times \vec{w}) = 2\vec{i}\text{.}\)
