Here,
\begin{equation*} \vec{u}_T = \frac{(-20+21)^{\circ}C}{16.9 \ {\rm{km}}} \vec{i} + \frac{(-21+20)^{\circ}C}{37.7 \ {\rm{km}}} \vec{j}. \end{equation*}
The advection is
\begin{align*} -\vec{v}_H \cdot \vec{u}_T = \mathstrut \amp - \left(18.5 \frac{m}{\rm{hr}} \vec{i} - 32.0 \frac{m}{\rm{hr}}\vec{j}\right) \cdot \left( \frac{1^{\circ}C}{16.9 \ {\rm{km}}} \vec{i} + \frac{-1^{\circ}C}{37.7 \ {\rm{km}}} \vec{j}\right) \\ = \mathstrut \amp -1.9 \frac{^{\circ}C}{\rm{hr}} \end{align*}

\begin{align*} T_{fcst} = \mathstrut \amp -20^{\circ}C + (0.5 \ {\rm{hr}})\left(-1.9 \ \frac{^{\circ}C}{\rm{hr}}\right) \\ = \mathstrut \amp -20.95^{\circ}C. \end{align*}
