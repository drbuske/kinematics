  3. \begin{align*} \frac{\partial{\rm{WBGT}}}{\partial T}(30,95) \approx \mathstrut \amp \frac{38^{\circ}C - 35^{\circ}C}{31^{\circ}C - 29^{\circ}C}\\ = \mathstrut \amp 1.5\frac{^{\circ}C}{^{\circ}C} \end{align*}
    We take this to mean that when the temperature is \(30^{\circ}\) and the relative humidity is 95%, then when the temperature increases \(1^{\circ}C\) and the relative humidity stays constant, the WBGT measure increases by \(1.5^{\circ}C\text{.}\)
