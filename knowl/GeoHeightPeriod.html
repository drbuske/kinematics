Example 5.1.2. Geopotential Height is Periodic.

The period for geopotential height data is \(T=360^{\circ}\) since, when on any line of latitude, moving that many degrees longitude to the east will yield the same height data. The angular frequency \(\omega_0 = \frac{2\pi}{360^{\circ}} = 1\) in this case.
