Atmospheric pressure is a function of location \((x,y,z)\) in space and of time \(t\text{.}\) That is, \(p = p(x,y,z,t)\text{.}\) A drone is located at various locations \((x,y,z)\) at time \(t\text{.}\) That is, \(x\text{,}\) \(y\text{,}\) and \(z\) are all functions of time \(t\) for the drone. Give an expression involving partial derivatives for the instantaneous rate of change of pressure \(\frac{dp}{dt}\) on this drone.
